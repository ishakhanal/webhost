LetsBid

Contracter: Person who has some contract(job/task) to be fulfilled.
Bidders: People who are capable of fulfilling that contract( willing to do that job/task)
Stated Price: The average price which contractor is ready to pay for his contract fulfilment.
Bidding Price: The price that is bidded by the bidder for the contract.

Working Mechanism

For a particular task, contracter post his contract and gives a stated price for the contract along with the deadline. The contract is displayed to the bidder along with deadline but the stated price is hidden.The contractor pays the stated price to the system before publishing it publicly.

The bidder should bid the price before crossing the deadline. When bidders see a contract posted, they submit their bidding price accordingly. The bidders name and bidding amount is not displayed in the post until the deadline is met. Once deadline is crossed, the list of bidders along with their bidding amount is published publicly. That list is forwarded to the contractor to choose the appropriate bidder for the contract fulfilment.

Now, the selected bidders bidding amount and stated price is compared. If the amount is less than the stated price , the remaining amount is refunded to the contractor. If the amount is higher than the stated price, contractor needs to pay the remaining amount to the system. After that, the bidder contact information is provided to the contractor. Once his contract gets fulfilled and contractor approves its fulfilment, system pays the bidded amount to the bidder.
